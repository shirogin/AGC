How did it start?

It all started a decennium Ago (in 2010) ,when a small group of young and passionate Micro Club game developers had the brilliant idea of organizing a Game developing competition, in an attempt to provide a platform where the game dev community can shine and attract new people. That how AGC came to life Kind Of, back then Micro Club Had Microsoft as a partner, and since Microsoft had a tool/platform dedicated to game development called XNA, the competition was called XNA game challenge, and so the first edition occurred later on in April 2011.

The AGC journey

Starting as XNA game challenge in April 2011 and being rebranded as Algeria Game challenge in 2013, The competition occurred every year, which adds up to a total of 9 editions.
